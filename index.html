Markus "Yama"zaki Thornsey

“Despite being born in Brazil he was raised in Japan under a different name and even was formerly one of the most respected and feared yakuza men of his clan. Recruited by his strategic capabilities and most importantly vicious nature. Any and all records of him were redacted due to the gruesomeness depicted in them.”
-In-game biography

Markus Yamazaki Thornsey, also known as Yama, is an operator of the Warsaw Pact faction featured in Call of Duty: Black Ops Cold War and Call of Duty: Warzone. Yama was released with Season __ as part of the Battle Pass. He is an antagonist in the Black Ops Storyline in Call of Duty: Warzone.
